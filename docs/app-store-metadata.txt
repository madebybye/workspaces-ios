DESCRIPTION (~1,450/4,000 chars)

Explore the workspaces of modern creators.

Workspaces is the companion app to workspaces.xyz — the weekly tour of real desk setups from designers, founders, and builders, read by 21,000+ people. A new issue lands every Saturday.

EVERY SETUP HAS A STORY
Step inside 500+ real home offices and studios. Each issue is a photo tour with an interview: who they are, what they make, and the thinking behind their space.

SEE THE GEAR BEHIND THE WORK
Every desk, chair, display, and keyboard is catalogued. Tap any item to see every setup that uses it, or browse the ranked list of the most-featured gear across the archive. Only gear real people actually use — no sponsored placements.

THE WHOLE ARCHIVE, OFFLINE
Every issue since 2020 is cached on your device. Read anywhere and search everything — names, interviews, gear, and tags — instantly, even without a connection.

MADE FOR READING
An editorial, magazine-style design with full-bleed photography. Save setups to your list, browse curated collections, pinch to zoom on every photo, and share the shots that inspire you. Beautiful on iPhone and iPad, in light and dark.

— A new setup every Saturday, with a gentle weekly reminder
— 500+ issues, fully searchable and offline
— Browse by tag, collection, or gear
— Save your favorites
— No accounts, no tracking

Workspaces is an unofficial companion to workspaces.xyz. Gear links may be affiliate links.
